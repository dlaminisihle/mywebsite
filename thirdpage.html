Marinated Vegetables
Deluxe

100% of health
deluxe freshness



Ingredients & Measurements
  • 1 head cauliflower
  • 1 bunch broccoli, with stems
  • 3 stalks celery
  • 6 medium carrots
  • 1 1⁄2 cups cherry tomatoes
  • 5 green onions
  • (10 ounce) can whole mushrooms
  • (14 ounce) can pitted black olives
  • (14 ounce) can miniature corn cobs, drained
  • (8 ounce) can water chestnuts, drained
  • green pepper, cut into bite size pieces
  • (8 ounce) bottle Italian dressing (I use Kraft)

Health and Nutrition
  • Calories from Fat 40 51%
  • Sodium 320.3 mg 13%
  • Total Carbohydrate 9.7 g 3%
  • Dietary Fiber 2.9 g 11%
  • Sugars 3.5 g 14%
  • Protein 2.1 g 4%

How To Cook/Prepar.

Break or cut vegetables into bite size pieces and place in large bowl. Pour dressing over vegetables, mix and refrigerate overnight. Stir occasionally.
